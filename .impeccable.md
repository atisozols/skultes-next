## Design Context

### Users
Gym members on their phones. Primary use is at the gym (checking in, viewing sessions, verifying identity) and at home for booking the private gym ahead of time. Latvian-speaking audience.

### Brand Personality
Premium, clean, confident. The interface should feel like a tool that respects the user's time — no clutter, no unnecessary decoration. Every element earns its place.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Tone**: Apple-inspired restraint. Apple Health app density and clarity. Information-rich without feeling busy.
- **Theme**: Dark mode only. Black background (#000), dark containers (#1c1c1e), red accent (#ff2429). Monochromatic with red as the sole accent — used sparingly for emphasis.
- **Anti-references**: Colorful, playful, or busy interfaces. No gradients, no multi-color palettes. The brand palette is black, white, gray, red — nothing else.
- **Fonts**: InterVariable (body), BakbakOne (display headings). Already established, do not change.

### Design Principles
1. **Density over decoration** — Pack information tight, use spacing for hierarchy, never add visual elements that don't carry meaning.
2. **Red means something** — The accent color is reserved for actionable items, active states, and critical information. Overuse dilutes it.
3. **One-hand, one-glance** — Everything must work with a thumb on a phone screen. Key info visible without scrolling. Touch targets generous.
4. **Motion with purpose** — Framer Motion transitions for state changes, not for decoration. Quick ease-out curves. Respect reduced-motion.
5. **Latvian-native** — All copy in Latvian. Short, direct labels. No filler text.
